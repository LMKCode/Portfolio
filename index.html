About Me

Intro

Hi, I’m Lukas — a curious explorer in the world of programming with a deep fascination for technology and its impact on our future. My journey began during my Media Conception studies, where I first got hands-on experience with web and game development, bringing ideas to life through code. What started as an exciting challenge quickly turned into a true passion — especially for artificial intelligence. Even before AI tools like ChatGPT became mainstream, I was captivated by the immense potential of this technology. But it’s not just the possibilities that intrigue me; it’s also the profound philosophical questions that come with it.

Vision

Today, I’m certain that I want to keep learning everything I can about this field to unlock endless opportunities for the future. For me, programming isn’t just a technical skill — it’s a powerful tool that can be used for great good or, unfortunately, for harm. My ultimate goal is to use my skills to drive innovation in areas like healthcare, security, and space exploration.

Strengths

Along the way, I benefit not only from my technical knowledge but also from my interdisciplinary background. While I may still lack deep IT expertise due to my Media Conception studies, I see this as a strength. My ability to think beyond the boundaries of technology helps me approach complex problems from a broader perspective. My experience in UX/UI design, web analytics, marketing, and project planning translates seamlessly into the IT and coding world. It allows me to not only implement projects technically but also consider the user perspective and strategic planning.

Skills

Of course, I’m still at the beginning of my journey, but I’ve already gained valuable experience. During my studies, I worked on early projects using TypeScript, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. As my interest in machine learning grew, I started (just recently) experimenting with Python and developed small applications. I also had the chance to work with exciting libraries and tools such as Three.js, GSAP, PixiJS, Pygame, MongoDB and the open-source game engine FUDGE (Furtwangen University Didactic Game Editor). Every new project and every challenge shows me how limitless the possibilities truly are.

Motivation

I firmly believe that the journey is the reward — and I’m excited to keep learning and growing. What drives me most is the idea that technology can be a force for positive change, solving real-world problems and pushing the boundaries of what’s possible. I want to develop solutions that not only advance industries but also improve lives. Whether it’s making healthcare more accessible, enhancing digital & physical security, or contributing to the exploration of the unknown, I see programming as a way to build a better future. The more I learn, the more I realize how much there is still to discover — and that’s exciting!
